Step 1: Visit the Website

  • Open your browser and go to the official site URL: https://steelpredictor.example.com.
  • The homepage provides an overview of the prediction system and its capabilities.

Step 2: Access the Prediction Tool

  • Click the "Use Model" button in the navigation bar or on the homepage.
  • You'll be redirected to the dedicated Prediction page.

Step 3: Upload Input Data

  • Upload your Excel file (.xlsx) with chemical composition and process data.
  • Required fields include: %C, %Mn, %Cr, %Ni, Input Thickness, etc.
  • Ensure column headers are clean (avoid special characters or merged cells).

Step 4: Run Predictions

  • Click the "Predict" button once your data is uploaded.
  • The model will process your data on the backend and return predictions for:
    • 0.2% Yield Strength (MPa)
    • Ultimate Tensile Strength (MPa)
    • Elongation at 50mm G.L (%)

Step 5: Review & Download Results

  • View the results directly on-screen.
  • Optionally, click "Download Results" to get the predictions in Excel format.
  • Visual graphs (like bar charts or scatter plots) may be available for comparative analysis.

Step 6: Feedback or Support

  • Click the "Get in Touch" tab in the navigation menu.
  • Submit feedback, technical queries, or suggestions using the provided form.
